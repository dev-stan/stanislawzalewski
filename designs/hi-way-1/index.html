GOOD QUESTIONS

Learner questions, answered

How many lessons will I need?

It depends on your experience, but most learners need 10–20 hours. After your first assessment we give you an honest estimate — never an inflated one.

Auto or manual?

We teach both. An automatic licence is quicker and simpler; a manual licence lets you drive anything. Not sure? We'll talk it through on your first call — there's no wrong answer.

Whose car do I use?

Ours — dual-controlled, fully insured and maintained. You're welcome to use it for your VicRoads test too, which is what most of our learners do.

What happens on test day?

We pick you up early for a warm-up drive around the test route, take care of the paperwork, and hand you the keys calm and ready.

Any lock-in or upfront cost?

None. Pay lesson by lesson if that suits you. Packages are there to save you money, never to lock you in.
