Frequently asked questions

Do you offer both auto and manual lessons?

Yes — our instructors teach both automatic and manual transmission, so you can learn on whichever suits your goals.

Do you pick up from home, school or work?

All three. Tell us where you'll be and your instructor comes to your door — home, school or workplace across Melbourne's northern suburbs.

How many lessons will I need before my test?

It depends on your experience. After your first relaxed assessment drive we'll give you an honest plan — how many lessons, what to focus on and when you'll be test-ready.

Can I book a package or pay as I go?

Both. Lesson packages work out cheaper per hour, but you're welcome to pay as you go one lesson at a time. Gift vouchers are available too.

Do you provide the car for my VicRoads test?

Yes. You can use our dual-control car for the test, and we'll warm up on the real route beforehand so nothing feels unfamiliar.

What suburbs do you service?

Melbourne's northern suburbs — from Heidelberg and Greensborough up through Bundoora, Mill Park, South Morang, Mernda and out to Hurstbridge. See the full list below.
